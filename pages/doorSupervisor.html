Door Supervisor Course

FIELDZ Training's Level 2 Door Supervisor course is a recognized qualification that satisfies the standards established by the Security Industry Authority (SIA). It is in accordance with the relevant SIA Specifications for Learning and Qualifications, providing candidates with the necessary information and skills to apply for a SIA license and pursue employment as a door supervisor.

Potential students must first complete some distance learning prior to the first day of instruction in order to complete the 6-day Level 2 Door Supervisor course. The initial phase of distance learning serves as a prelude, providing students with a fundamental understanding and ensuring a more efficient and productive training experience during the in-person sessions..

A current and valid First Aid or Emergency First Aid certificate that meets the requirements of the Health and Safety (First Aid) Regulations 1981, as specified by the SIA, is also required for individuals enrolled in Door Supervision or Security Officer training. This certification must remain valid for a minimum of one year following the commencement of the security course.

The SIA emphasizes the importance of learners obtaining the requisite First Aid or Emergency First Aid certifications to ensure a more secure and organized work environment.
