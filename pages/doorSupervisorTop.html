Door Supervisor TOP UP Course

In fact, the Level 2 Door Supervisor Top Up course, which is offered by FIELDZ Training,  is of considerable significance to two specific groups:

  • SIA Door Supervisor license holders who plan to renew their licenses after October 1, 2021.
  • Applicants for SIA licenses who have already obtained a Door Supervisor qualification prior to April 1, 2021 and submit their applications after October 1, 2021.

In order to satisfy the necessity for successful completion of the Level 2 Door Supervisor course and new license applications, the Security Industry Authority (SIA) has revised the requirements for both of these groups.

In order to complete the Level 2 Door Supervisor course within the designated six-day timeframe, candidates must engage in some form of distance learning prior to attending the first day of the course. Learners must possess the foundational knowledge acquired through this pre-course distance learning component in order to guarantee a more concentrated and effective training experience during the in-person sessions. The following requirement enables candidates to acquire the necessary skills and knowledge to serve as a competent and accountable Door Supervisor.
