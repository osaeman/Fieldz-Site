About Fieldz Training

Fieldz Training is an organization that provides effective training programs for various sectors, such as education, business, security, first aid, and health and safety. Additionally, we have a proven history of providing top-notch security services to businesses, community, and nonprofit organizations.

Our instructors possess over a decade of education and relevant experience. We partner with experienced associate trainers to ensure the highest quality of our critical training programs.

Fieldz Staff are valued at Training. We rigorously recruit top candidates for each position to ensure excellent security services in the UK. Officers are polite and customer-focused. They undergo extensive training to meet our organization's and clients' high standards and ensure long-term success. Our officers undergo rigorous vetting and screening according to British Standards. Individuals must provide references and identity verification for the past five years of employment.

Looking for top-notch security services? Visit our website. Through our commitment to excellence and social impact, we aim to improve and secure the future for individuals, businesses, and communities.
